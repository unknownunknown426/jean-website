The Joy of Singing: The happiness Jean Rica derives from singing is unparalleled. The act of singing, whether alone or with friends, serves as a constant source of positivity. Conclusion: Jean Rica A. Sinangote, the working student from Tangub City, exemplifies the power of passion and resilience. Amidst the demands of academia and work, she has discovered the therapeutic effects of singing, turning it into an integral part of her life. As she continues her journey at Blancia College Foundation, INC., and navigates the complexities of being a working student, Jean Rica's love for music remains a constant, providing her with solace, joy, and a melody to accompany her on the path to success.
